Overview

Purpose

The main purpose of the Rafting website is to provide information about our rafting company and what our company has to offer. To provide a chill browsing experience to the visitor searching for available rafting sessions for individuals or groups. They can also find answers to frequently asked questions and contact us to schedule an appointment.

Audience

Our target audience are adventurous groups that are interested in extreme physical activities as well as families looking for fun and safe activities. We provide a service for individuals that are interested in a safe rafting adventure that are affordable yet memorable.
